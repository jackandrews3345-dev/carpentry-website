About Our Craft

Dedicated to excellence in woodworking and carpentry

Expert Carpenter and Master Craftsman in The Bahamas

With years of experience in fine carpentry and woodworking, I bring passion and precision to every project. From custom furniture to home renovations, each piece is crafted with attention to detail and built to last in The Bahamas' tropical climate.

Located in the beautiful Bahamas, I serve clients across Nassau, Freeport, Paradise Island, and all major Bahamian islands with quality craftsmanship that combines traditional Caribbean woodworking techniques with modern innovation.
