🔨 How to Add Your Carpentry Images

This guide shows you how to replace the placeholder content with your actual carpentry photos and videos.

📸 Step 1: Prepare Your Images

Image Requirements:

  • Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
  • Size: Minimum 1200px wide for best quality
  • Aspect Ratio: 4:3 or 16:9 works best for galleries
  • File Size: Under 2MB each for fast loading

🗂️ Step 2: Organize Your Files

Suggested folder structure:

assets/images/ ├── furniture/ │ ├── custom-table-1.jpg │ ├── dining-set-2.jpg │ └── bookshelf-3.jpg ├── renovations/ │ ├── kitchen-before.jpg │ ├── kitchen-after.jpg │ └── bathroom-remodel.jpg ├── custom-work/ │ ├── built-in-storage.jpg │ ├── custom-shelving.jpg │ └── cabinet-doors.jpg └── profile/ └── your-photo.jpg

✏️ Step 3: Update the HTML

BEFORE (Current Placeholder):

<div class="gallery-item" data-category="furniture"> <div class="image-placeholder"> <i class="fas fa-image"></i> <p>Custom Table</p> </div> <div class="gallery-overlay"> <i class="fas fa-expand"></i> </div> </div>

AFTER (With Real Image):

<div class="gallery-item" data-category="furniture"> <img src="assets/images/furniture/custom-table-1.jpg" alt="Custom dining table made from local Bahamian wood"> <div class="gallery-overlay"> <i class="fas fa-expand"></i> </div> </div>

🎬 Step 4: Add Videos

BEFORE (Video Placeholder):

<div class="video-item"> <div class="video-placeholder"> <i class="fas fa-play-circle"></i> <p>Furniture Making Process</p> </div> </div>

AFTER (With Real Video):

<div class="video-item"> <video controls poster="assets/images/video-thumbnail.jpg"> <source src="assets/videos/table-making.mp4" type="video/mp4"> <source src="assets/videos/table-making.webm" type="video/webm"> Your browser doesn't support video playback. </video> </div>

👤 Step 5: Add Your Profile Photo

In the About section, replace:

<div class="image-placeholder"> <i class="fas fa-user"></i> <p>Your Photo Here</p> </div>

With:

<img src="assets/images/profile/your-photo.jpg" alt="Professional carpenter in the Bahamas" style="width: 300px; height: 300px; border-radius: 50%; object-fit: cover;">

🎨 Step 6: Image Optimization Tips

For best results:

  • Compress images: Use tools like TinyPNG or JPEG-Optimizer
  • Consistent lighting: Edit photos to have similar brightness
  • Professional angles: Show your work from the best perspectives
  • Alt text: Always include descriptive alt attributes for SEO
  • File naming: Use descriptive names like "mahogany-dining-table.jpg"

📱 Mobile Considerations

  • Test images on mobile devices - they should load quickly
  • Consider creating smaller versions for mobile (responsive images)
  • Ensure text overlays are readable on small screens
  • Videos should have appropriate mobile controls

🔍 SEO Best Practices

Alt Text Examples:

  • Good: "Custom mahogany dining table for Nassau home"
  • Better: "Handcrafted 8-person mahogany dining table with traditional joinery"
  • Best: "Custom mahogany dining table crafted by Bahamas carpenter using traditional mortise and tenon joints"

🎯 Quick Checklist

Before publishing:

  • ✅ All images are under 2MB
  • ✅ Alt text is descriptive and includes location keywords
  • ✅ Images show your best work
  • ✅ Videos have poster images (thumbnails)
  • ✅ File names are SEO-friendly
  • ✅ Test on mobile and desktop
  • ✅ All placeholders are replaced
